About Me

Hi, I’m Abdulsomad Abdulsalam — a passionate, self-taught developer with a growing love for building practical, user-focused web applications. I’ve explored different areas of tech, from Python and Django to Node.js, Express, and React, and I enjoy bringing ideas to life through clean, functional code. I’ve worked on several full-stack projects, including TaskMaster, a modern task management app, and REVIEWA, a platform designed to simplify how people share reviews of products and services. My projects reflect my goal of creating real-world solutions that are both efficient and accessible. I’m currently focused on deepening my expertise in backend development and full-stack engineering, exploring how to build scalable, well-structured systems. I also love learning new technologies, improving my craft daily, and documenting my progress to inspire other self-taught learners. When I’m not coding, I enjoy exploring ideas at the intersection of technology, learning, and community, and I’m always open to collaboration and growth opportunities.
